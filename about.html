Who are we?

A social enterprise delivering bespoke digital solutions for your business needs.

We take an active responsibility in operations and deliver quality results at competitive prices. We invest in your growth, and our initiative and work frees will free you to focus on your core business issues.

As a social enterprise, we also contribute to local community development.

OUR MISSION

“Your Growth, Our Success”

It is equally applicable to our talents as well to our clients. We truly believe that placing the growth of our Clients and our talents at the forefront, will surely ensure our success.

Our Story

Navaneethan has always had a passion for working in the field of social entrepreneurship despite coming from a farming family. After graduating, he immediately started working as an accountant. Aravinth, one of his friends, informed him about the opportunity to provide data processing solutions in order to support his objectives.As a result, they both decided to launch their own firm in order to offer those services.However, Navaneethan's commitment to raising the living standards of rural residents persisted. As an entrepreneur, he also sought to give job possibilities to those with disabilities and students who could work while attending college.

Aravinth was born in a war-zone in Sri Lanka, and he spent 24 years of his life as a war-child and refugee. His craving to uphold those who went through the same life experiences propelled him to co-found DreamSpace Academy - a non-profit social enterprise empowering underprivileged communities in war-affected regions of Sri Lanka.

Despite the fact that Aravinth and Navaneethan were engaged in separate endeavours, their shared passion for social entrepreneurship brought them together and sparked the creation of Zerosec Solutions as a social venture in 2015. They began it in a 15 sq.ft office space with just Navaneethan and a moderator, as the only two employees. The first year was spent on developing demo solutions to demonstrate how everything worked and persuade clients to test it out. In 2018, the company relocated to a 250 square foot office space with 10 employees. It took two entrepreneur’s 7 years of unwavering perseverance to make it as Zerosec Solutions Pvt. Ltd. in 2022.

Zerosec Solutions has been offering round-the-clock assistance since 2015, as we promised to one of the clients that offer us the very first opportunity. Despite the countless real-world issues we have encountered, such as pandemics and natural disasters, our perseverance and dedication to our work have never wavered. Additionally, even throughout the holiday season, our 24/7 workforce offers unflagging services.

OUR COMMITMENT

After almost a decade of living in urban clusters and observing the need to recalibrate the development idea, we at zerosec solutions have made the following commitment:

  • Reverse the trend of urban agglomeration which is one of the main causes for rural poverty.
  • Upskill rural youth to global standards.
  • Deliver cost-effective services to global companies.
  • Promote community development.

Equal Opportunity Employer

We are an equal opportunity provider and actively strive to build diversity in our teams, the years we have supported:

  • Differently-abled talents by providing work from home facility.
  • Part time work for college students who are first-generation graduates.
  • Opportunity for homemakers to build their career and other persons in need.
  • Equal employment opportunities irrespective of gender.

Empowerment

Some of our notable initiatives in supporting the local community as well as our talents are:

  • We have set-up a borrowing library in our office to expose our talents to ideas from across the world Mentorship program to working students.
  • Fast-tracking talent to managerial positions.
