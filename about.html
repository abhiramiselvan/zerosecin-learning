Who Are We?

A social enterprise delivering bespoke digital solutions for your business needs.

Established in 2015, Zerosec Solutions deploy real-time & proficient Data, Business & Legal Solutions

Our Mission

“Your Growth, Our Success”

It is equally applicable to our talents as well to our clients. We truly believe that placing the growth of our Clients and our talents at the forefront, will surely ensure our success.




Ourstory

  • We Started in 2015

    Started Zerosec Solution as a social enterprise in 15 sq.ft office space with just Two employees

  • 2016

    Developed demo solutions to demonstrate

  • 2018

    Relocated to a 250 sq.ft office space with 10 employees

  • 2021

    Extended to 375 sq.ft office space with amenities such as library

  • 2022

OUR COMMITMENT

After almost a decade of living in urban clusters and observing the need to recalibrate the development idea, we at zerosec solutions have made the following commitment:

  • Reverse the trend of urban agglomeration which is one of the main causes for rural poverty.
  • Upskill rural youth to global standards.
  • Deliver cost-effective services to global companies.
  • Promote community development.

Equal Opportunity Employer

We are an equal opportunity provider and actively strive to build diversity in our teams, Over the years we have supported:

  • Differently-abled talents by providing work from home facility.
  • Part time work for college students who are first-generation graduates.
  • Opportunity for homemakers to build their career and other persons in need.
  • Equal employment opportunities irrespective of gender.

Empowerment

Some of our notable initiatives in supporting the local community as well as our talents are:

  • We have set-up a borrowing library in our office to expose our talents to ideas from across the world Mentorship program to working students.
  • Fast-tracking talent to managerial positions.
  • Organising community events.
  • Financial assistance to 40+ first-generation graduates etc.
  • Supports women empowerment by providing work from home option to homemakers .

Our Infrastructure

We have a 2300 square feet office space with adequate ventilation and other basic amenities. We ensure that our employees have a trouble-free working environment.

  • Uninterrupted power supply with backup power sources.
  • Hassle free working with highly configured systems and high speed internet.
  • We've also set up a library for employees to relax in.
  • Providing food and accommodation for 24/7 support team.
  • Allocated a separate area for employees for stress-relieving activities.
Social Responsibility

  • As a social enterprise, we are always concerned about our society. We use solar energy for all of its electricity needs to minimise greenhouse gas emissions and thus protect the environment.
  • Zerosec Solutions organises mentoring programs for school & college students to foster creativity and imagination in young minds and inculcate skills.
  • Organises awareness programs in nearby rural areas to assist students in better shape their future
  • We have partnered with Nature and Geo Society, an NGO that aims to create conservation awareness and education among youth and the general public.
    • The society also organises camps to encourage children to continue their studies in forest, wildlife, ecology, and biodiversity conservation. The society's experience has been to bring tribal and local communities together to build long-term forest-related solutions by leveraging their generational knowledge. They also hold rallies to encourage people to participate in and promote afforestation efforts.
    • The NGO encourages and expands green cover in government schools through Eco-Clubs.
    • Also, out-bound learning sessions were conducted for kids in Anamalai Tiger Reserve.
    • We at Zerosec Solutions work with and support Nature and the Geo Society in their mission.
